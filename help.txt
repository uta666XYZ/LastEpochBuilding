---[Help File Introduction]

This file contains a list of shortcuts and other misc information about Last Epoch Building functionality.
While holding shift, scrolling in the help section or version history will jump to the previous/next section.

Press F1 anytime to open this help.

---[Other Notable Things]

Adding ^ and then a number or hex code before text will change the colour of the text (e.g. ^^77 will make the following text white until the next colour marker is set).

---[Party Tab]

The Party tab allows you to import support characters and have their auras and curses apply to your build.

To import a build, it must be exported with "Export support" enabled in the Import / Export tab, and must be imported in the Party tab.
You can import a specific type like aura or curse, or import everything.
You can also set it to append to a section rather than replace it (curses and links are always replaced if the new one has a curse / link).

This does not add the auras, curses or links into the Skills tab, but they do show up on the Calcs tab under "aura and buff skills" as well as "curses and debuffs" respectively.
They also show other effects they add.

Auras with the highest effect will take priority.
Your curses will take priority over a support's.

---[Skills Tab]

The Skills tab allows you to plan out the skills you will use for your character. To access this tab, click "Skills" in the tab row at the top left of the screen, between "Tree" and "Items".

From here, you can view all of the skills in your build.

Skill sets can be thought of as collections of skills. There is a select box above that lets you switch between them.
A great way to use them is to create skill sets for different level brackets, allowing you to plan out which skills you can run at certain points in your levelling progression.
They are also a nice way to showcase different skill options for a build. One set could show the skills you run to maximize DPS, while another could show a better setup for survivability or mapping. Click Manage next to the select box to add, remove, rename, and copy skill sets.

Toggling the "Enabled" checkbox will enable or disable that skill from your build. You can also use the "Include in Full DPS" checkbox to include or exclude that skill from the Full DPS calculation displayed on the left panel.
This can be useful for seeing how much damage you are getting from specific skills, as well as from specific groups of skills in your gear.

---[Items Tab]

The Items tab in Last Epoch Building allows you to plan out the gear you will use for your character. To access the Items tab, click on the "Items" tab located at the top of the screen.

From here, you can view all of the gear slots for your character, such as your weapon, chest armour, and boots. To add an item to a slot, click the arrow button on the right side of the slot. This will bring up a list of all the items available in your build, which are visible in the "All items" window.
If you wish to add more items, you can copy and paste items using Ctrl + C and Ctrl + V into the "Shared items" window. Additionally, you can craft items using the "Craft item..." button on the top, or create completely custom items using the "Create custom..." option.

The item sets section located at the top of the screen can be used to save multiple sets of items and switch between them. You can add, remove, rename, and copy new sets using the "Manage..." button.
